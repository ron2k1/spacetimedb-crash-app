# Inbox Triage

This skill helps you get through a pile of messages without reading every one from scratch. You paste in a batch of emails or messages, and it sorts them by how urgent they are and drafts replies you can quickly review and send.

The steps it follows are:

- Read each message and group it by priority, such as needs a reply now, can wait, and no action needed.
- Give a one-line summary of each message so you know what it is about at a glance.
- Draft a short, polite reply for the messages that need one, matching a normal everyday tone.
- Point out anything that looks sensitive or important, like a deadline or a request for money, so you slow down and check before sending.

The point is to turn a stressful inbox into a short to-do list. You stay in control, since the drafts are suggestions you read and approve, not messages it sends on its own. Nothing leaves your hands until you say so.
