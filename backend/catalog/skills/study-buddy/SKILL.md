# Study Buddy

This skill turns your study material into practice you can actually use. You give it your notes, a chapter, or a set of terms, and it builds practice questions and flashcards so you can test yourself instead of just rereading.

Here is what it does, step by step:

- Read your material and find the key ideas, terms, and facts worth remembering.
- Write a set of practice questions in a mix of styles, such as multiple choice, short answer, and true or false.
- Provide the correct answer for each question, with a short explanation so you learn from your mistakes.
- Build flashcards as simple question-and-answer pairs you can run through quickly before a quiz or exam.

It is meant to feel like a patient study partner. You decide how many questions you want and how hard they should be, and it keeps the focus on the material you gave it rather than wandering off topic. Use it to check what you really know before the test, not just what you have read.
